15+ Years Experience In Power Transmission Systems

Power Transmission Systems is a leading name in the renewable energy sector, committed to delivering efficient and sustainable energy solutions. With decades of experience in solar power installations, transmission systems, and infrastructure development, we aim to revolutionize energy consumption. Our mission is to create a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future by reducing dependency on conventional power sources. Our Services

What We Deliver

Ground-Mounted Solar Solutions

We specialize in designing and implementing large-scale ground-mounted solar systems that are robust and efficient. These systems are ideal for industries and institutions with ample open spaces, ensuring high energy yield and minimal environmental impact. Our ground-mounted solutions include state-of-the-art technology and durable designs to withstand diverse weather conditions.

Rooftop Solar Solutions

Harness the potential of your rooftop with our cutting-edge solar installations. Our rooftop systems are customized to maximize energy generation while ensuring aesthetic integration with your building. These solutions are perfect for reducing electricity bills in residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.

Operation & Maintenance

We offer a full suite of operation and maintenance services to ensure your solar systems perform optimally throughout their lifespan. From regular inspections to system upgrades and troubleshooting, our expert team ensures seamless energy production and minimal downtime.
